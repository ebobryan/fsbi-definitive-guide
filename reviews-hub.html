Where We Can Improve

We value all feedback, especially the critical kind. Here are common themes and our direct responses.

"They won't invest in my idea!"

"They're good as long as you have money, like any business! But they won't invest anything!!!! Even to make MONEY!!!"

üí¨ Our Response

We understand this perspective. For Sale By Inventor is a service-based company, not an investment firm. This is intentional. It would be a conflict of interest for us to invest in some ideas and not others. Our fee-for-service model ensures that you retain 100% ownership and control of your idea. We provide the expertise; the financial risk and reward remain with you.

"They didn't get me a deal!"

"No one has responded to me yet. No phone call or email. I am concerned about this."

üí¨ Our Response

We can't guarantee results, and any company that does is not being truthful. The invention industry has a very low success rate. Our role is to create professional marketing materials and submit them to companies in your target industry. We provide you with a list of companies we contact, but we cannot force them to respond or make a deal.

"They stole my idea!"

"I told them my biggest concern was someone stealing my idea...They then told me they couldn't help me!"

üí¨ Our Response

We have never and will never steal an idea. We are bound by a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) with every person who contacts us. Every person who signs up receives a time-stamped copy of this NDA. All FSBI employees are required to sign NDAs. We do not develop inventions ourselves, and we have never profited from someone's invention without their consent.

"It costs too much!"

"I can't rate something that hasn't done anything for me but send me a bill."

üí¨ Our Response

We charge fees for our services, but we keep them reasonable compared to other companies and patent attorneys. We offer fixed fees with no obligation, so you know the full cost upfront. Our expertise helps streamline the patent process, which can save you money in the long run. We also offer a money-back promise for our research services.

Plain Talk

A Common Question

"Why Don't You Just Buy My Idea?"

We hear this a lot: "If my idea is so good, why don't you just run with it and we'll split it 90-10?"

"Ideas are like armpits: everyone has at least two, and they usually stink... unless you act on them."

An idea is just the starting point. The real value comes from execution: research, patenting, marketing, manufacturing, distribution. This requires significant investment. We are not Shark Tank. Why would anyone invest in your idea if you don't feel it's worth investing any of your own resources?
